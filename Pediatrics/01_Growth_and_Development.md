# GROWTH & DEVELOPMENT - NEET PG Study Material

## Definition
**Growth**: Increase in physical size (quantitative)
**Development**: Functional maturation (qualitative)

---

## Principles of Development

1. **Cephalocaudal**: Head to toe (head control → sitting → standing)
2. **Proximodistal**: Center to periphery (shoulder → elbow → wrist → fingers)
3. **Mass to specific**: Gross motor → Fine motor
4. **Continuous**: From conception to maturity
5. **Sequential**: Fixed order (cannot skip stages)
6. **Individual variation**: Rate varies (timing may differ)

**NEET PG**: Development follows predictable sequence but rate varies

---

## Assessment of Growth

### Weight

**Birth weight**: 2.5-4 kg (Average: **3.3 kg**)
**Low birth weight (LBW)**: <2.5 kg
**Very low birth weight (VLBW)**: <1.5 kg
**Extremely low birth weight (ELBW)**: <1 kg

**Weight Gain**:
- **Birth weight regained**: By **10 days**
- **Birth weight doubles**: By **4-5 months**
- **Birth weight triples**: By **12 months**
- **Birth weight quadruples**: By **2 years**

**Average weight gain**:
- **0-3 months**: 30g/day
- **3-6 months**: 20g/day
- **6-12 months**: 10g/day
- **1-3 years**: 2 kg/year
- **3-puberty**: 3-3.5 kg/year

**NEET PG High-Yield**:
- Birth weight **doubles** by 5 months
- Birth weight **triples** by 12 months
- Birth weight **quadruples** by 2 years

---

### Length/Height

**Birth length**: 50 cm (Average)

**Length/Height milestones**:
- **Birth length increases by 50%**: By **1 year** (75 cm)
- **Birth length doubles**: By **4 years** (100 cm)
- **Birth length triples**: By **13 years** (150 cm)

**Average growth**:
- **First year**: 25 cm
- **Second year**: 12.5 cm (half of 1st year)
- **Third year**: 9 cm
- **4 years-puberty**: 5-6 cm/year

**NEET PG**: Length at 1 year = 75 cm (1.5× birth length)

---

### Head Circumference (HC)

**Birth HC**: 35 cm (Average: 33-37 cm)

**Milestones**:
- **Equals chest circumference**: **1 year** (46 cm)
- **Chest > Head**: After 1 year

**Growth**:
- **First year**: 12 cm (2 cm/month for first 3 months, then 1 cm/month)
- **Second year**: 2.5 cm
- **3-5 years**: 1 cm/year

**Adult HC**: 54-55 cm

**NEET PG High-Yield**:
- **HC = CC at 1 year** (both 46 cm)
- **Microcephaly**: HC <2 SD below mean
- **Macrocephaly**: HC >2 SD above mean

---

### Chest Circumference (CC)

**Birth CC**: 32 cm (2-3 cm less than HC)

**Milestones**:
- **Equals HC**: **1 year** (46 cm)
- **Exceeds HC**: After 1 year

---

### Dentition

#### Primary (Deciduous) Teeth
**Total**: 20 teeth (10 upper + 10 lower)

**Eruption**:
- **First tooth**: 6 months (lower central incisor)
- **All 20 teeth**: 24-30 months (2-2.5 years)

**Formula for number of teeth**:
**Age in months - 6** (valid from 6-30 months)
Example: At 12 months → 12-6 = **6 teeth**

**Order of eruption**:
1. Lower central incisors (6 months)
2. Upper central incisors (8 months)
3. Upper lateral incisors (10 months)
4. Lower lateral incisors (12 months)
5. First molars (14 months)
6. Canines (18 months)
7. Second molars (24 months)

#### Permanent Teeth
**Total**: 32 teeth

**Eruption**:
- **First permanent tooth**: **6 years** (first molar - "6-year molar")
- **Last tooth**: 17-25 years (third molar - wisdom tooth)

**NEET PG**:
- **First deciduous tooth**: 6 months (lower central incisor)
- **Number of teeth formula**: Age (months) - 6
- **First permanent tooth**: 6 years (first molar)

## Developmental Milestones

### Gross Motor Development

| Age | Milestone |
|-----|-----------|
| **Birth** | Flexed posture |
| **1 month** | Lifts chin off bed (prone) |
| **2 months** | Lifts chest off bed (prone) |
| **3 months** | Head control (holds head steady) |
| **4 months** | Rolls prone to supine |
| **6 months** | Sits with support, Rolls supine to prone |
| **7-8 months** | Sits without support |
| **9 months** | Crawls, Stands with support |
| **10 months** | Creeps (hands & knees) |
| **12 months** | Stands without support, **Walks with one hand held** |
| **15 months** | **Walks alone** (independently) |
| **18 months** | Runs, Walks upstairs with help |
| **2 years** | Walks up & down stairs (2 feet per step), Runs well, Kicks ball |
| **3 years** | Stands on one foot (few seconds), Rides tricycle |
| **4 years** | Hops on one foot, Walks downstairs (alternating feet) |
| **5 years** | Skips |

**NEET PG High-Yield**:
- **3 months**: Head control
- **6 months**: Sits with support
- **9 months**: Stands with support
- **12 months**: Stands alone, walks with one hand held
- **15 months**: **Walks alone** (most important)

---

### Fine Motor & Adaptive Development

| Age | Milestone |
|-----|-----------|
| **Birth** | Grasp reflex |
| **3 months** | Hands open (no more fisting), Holds rattle |
| **4 months** | Reaches for objects |
| **6 months** | **Palmar grasp** (whole hand), Transfers objects hand to hand |
| **9 months** | **Immature pincer grasp** (thumb & index finger) |
| **12 months** | **Mature pincer grasp** (tips of thumb & index) |
| **15 months** | Builds tower of 2 cubes |
| **18 months** | Builds tower of 3-4 cubes, Scribbles spontaneously |
| **2 years** | Builds tower of 6 cubes, **Imitates vertical line** |
| **3 years** | Builds tower of 9 cubes, **Copies circle** |
| **4 years** | Builds tower of 10+ cubes, **Copies cross (+)** |
| **5 years** | **Copies triangle**, **Draws a person** (6 parts) |

**Tower of Cubes Formula**: **Age (years) × 3** cubes (approximate)
- 2 years: 6 cubes
- 3 years: 9 cubes
- 4 years: 12 cubes

**NEET PG**:
- **6 months**: Palmar grasp
- **9 months**: Immature pincer
- **12 months**: Mature pincer grasp

---

### Language Development

| Age | Milestone |
|-----|-----------|
| **Birth** | Cries |
| **2 months** | **Social smile** (smiles at mother) |
| **3 months** | Coos (vowel sounds: aaa, ooo) |
| **6 months** | Babbles (consonants: ba-ba, da-da - non-specific) |
| **9 months** | **Mama/Dada (non-specific)** |
| **12 months** | **1 word with meaning** (Mama/Dada - specific), Understands "No" |
| **15 months** | 4-6 words |
| **18 months** | 10-20 words, Points to body parts |
| **2 years** | **50 words**, **2-word phrases**, Refers to self by name |
| **3 years** | **250 words**, **3-word sentences**, Knows age & gender |
| **4 years** | **1500 words**, Tells stories, Asks questions |
| **5 years** | **2000+ words**, Complete sentences |

**NEET PG High-Yield**:
- **2 months**: Social smile
- **6 months**: Babbles
- **12 months**: 1 word with meaning
- **2 years**: 50 words, 2-word phrases
- **3 years**: 3-word sentences

## Development Assessment

### Denver Developmental Screening Test (DDST-II)
**Assesses**: 4 domains
1. Gross motor (25%)
2. Fine motor-adaptive (25%)
3. Language (25%)
4. Personal-social (25%)

**Age range**: 0-6 years

**NEET PG**: DDST-II assesses 4 domains equally

---

### Developmental Quotient (DQ)

**Formula**: DQ = (Developmental age / Chronological age) × 100

**Interpretation**:
- **Normal**: 85-115
- **Borderline**: 70-84
- **Delayed**: <70

---

## Red Flags in Development

### Motor
- No head control by **4 months**
- Not sitting by **9 months**
- Not walking by **18 months**
- Hand dominance before **18 months** (suggests hemiparesis)

### Language
- No babbling by **9 months**
- No single words by **18 months**
- No 2-word phrases by **2.5 years**

### Social
- No social smile by **3 months**
- No eye contact

**NEET PG**: Hand dominance before 18 months = Abnormal (suspect cerebral palsy)

---

## Primitive Reflexes

| Reflex | Appears | Disappears | How to Elicit | Response |
|--------|---------|------------|---------------|----------|
| **Moro** | Birth | 4-6 months | Sudden head drop | Abduction → Adduction of arms |
| **Grasp** | Birth | 3-4 months | Place finger in palm | Grasps finger |
| **Rooting** | Birth | 4 months | Touch cheek | Turns head to that side |
| **Sucking** | Birth | 4 months | Touch lips | Sucking movements |
| **ATNR** | Birth | 6 months | Turn head to one side | Fencing posture |
| **Stepping** | Birth | 2 months | Hold upright, feet touch surface | Stepping movements |
| **Parachute** | **8-9 months** | Persists | Sudden forward thrust | Arms extend forward |

**NEET PG High-Yield**:
- **Moro reflex**: Absent in Erb's palsy (affected side)
- **Parachute reflex**: Appears at 8-9 months (protective reflex)
- **ATNR**: Asymmetric tonic neck reflex (Fencing posture)
- **Persistence of primitive reflexes**: Suggests cerebral palsy

## Nutritional Status Assessment

### Weight for Age
- **Normal**: >80% of expected
- **Grade I malnutrition**: 71-80%
- **Grade II**: 61-70%
- **Grade III**: 51-60%
- **Grade IV**: <50%

### Mid-Upper Arm Circumference (MUAC)
**Age**: 6 months - 5 years

**Interpretation**:
- **Green (≥13.5 cm)**: Normal
- **Yellow (12.5-13.4 cm)**: Moderate malnutrition
- **Red (<12.5 cm)**: Severe malnutrition

**NEET PG**: MUAC <12.5 cm = Severe acute malnutrition (SAM)

## Immunization Schedule (IAP 2023-24)

### Birth
- **BCG**: Left deltoid, intradermal
- **OPV-0** (or IPV)
- **Hepatitis B-1**

### 6 Weeks
- **OPV-1**, **IPV-1**
- **DTP-1** (or DTaP)
- **Hib-1**
- **Hepatitis B-2**
- **PCV-1**
- **Rotavirus-1**

### 10 Weeks
- **OPV-2**, **IPV-2**
- **DTP-2**
- **Hib-2**
- **PCV-2**
- **Rotavirus-2**

### 14 Weeks
- **OPV-3**, **IPV-3**
- **DTP-3**
- **Hib-3**
- **Hepatitis B-3**
- **PCV-3**
- **Rotavirus-3**

### 6 Months
- **Influenza-1** (annual thereafter)

### 9-12 Months
- **MMR-1** (9-12 months)
- **Typhoid conjugate vaccine** (9-12 months)
- **Hepatitis A-1** (12 months)

### 12-15 Months
- **PCV booster**

### 15-18 Months
- **DTP booster-1**
- **IPV booster-1**
- **Hib booster**
- **MMR-2**
- **Varicella-1**

### 18-19 Months
- **Hepatitis A-2**

### 4-6 Years
- **DTP booster-2**
- **IPV booster-2**
- **MMR-3** (optional)
- **Varicella-2**
- **Typhoid booster**

### 10-12 Years
- **Tdap/Td**
- **HPV** (2-3 doses for girls)

**NEET PG High-Yield**:
- **BCG**: Given at birth, left deltoid, intradermal
- **OPV vs IPV**: IPV preferred (no vaccine-associated paralytic polio)
- **MMR**: Live vaccine, contraindicated in immunocompromised
- **PCV**: Pneumococcal conjugate vaccine (13-valent)
- **Rotavirus**: Live oral vaccine (maximum age 8 months for last dose)

---

## Vaccine-Preventable Diseases

| Vaccine | Disease Prevented |
|---------|-------------------|
| **BCG** | Tuberculosis (severe forms - meningitis, miliary) |
| **OPV/IPV** | Poliomyelitis |
| **DTP** | Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis |
| **Hib** | H. influenzae type B (meningitis, pneumonia, epiglottitis) |
| **Hepatitis B** | Hepatitis B |
| **PCV** | Pneumococcal infections |
| **Rotavirus** | Rotavirus gastroenteritis |
| **MMR** | Measles, Mumps, Rubella |
| **Varicella** | Chickenpox |
| **HPV** | Cervical cancer (Human papillomavirus) |

**NEET PG**: BCG protects against severe TB (meningitis, miliary), NOT pulmonary TB
